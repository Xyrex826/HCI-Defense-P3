Overview

The Garden of Malasag Eco-Tourism Village is a unique and picturesque destination located in Cagayan de Oro City, Philippines. It serves as a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and eco-friendly tourism.

Attractions and Features:

  • The eco-village features beautifully landscaped gardens, showcasing a variety of native plants and flowers, which are both educational and visually appealing.
  • Visitors can explore the rich biodiversity of the area while enjoying the tranquility of the gardens.

Cultural Experience:

  • he Garden of Malasag highlights local culture and traditions, offering visitors insights into the indigenous communities of the region.
  • Cultural programs and activities are often organized, allowing visitors to engage with local artisans, performers, and craftspeople.

Lodging and Facilities:

  • he eco-tourism village provides accommodations, including rustic cottages and lodges that are designed to blend with the natural landscape. This allows visitors to experience comfort while being close to nature.
  • Amenities such as dining facilities and recreational areas are available, allowing guests to relax and enjoy local cuisine.

Conclusion

The Garden of Malasag Eco-Tourism Village is a valuable destination for those looking to connect with nature and learn about the rich cultural heritage of the Cagayan de Oro area. Its commitment to sustainability, coupled with its serene environment and engaging activities, makes it an excellent choice for eco-conscious travelers, families, and anyone seeking a refreshing escape from urban life. Whether exploring the gardens, enjoying local delicacies, or participating in cultural activities, visitors to the Garden of Malasag can expect a memorable and enriching experience.
