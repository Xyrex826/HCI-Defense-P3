Overview

Mapawa Nature Park, which includes attractions such as Mintugsok Falls, is a popular eco-tourism destination in Cagayan de Oro City, Philippines. The park is known for its rich biodiversity, scenic landscapes, and various outdoor activities.

Natural Attractions

  • Mintugsok Falls, one of the main highlights of the park, is a stunning waterfall cascading down rocky cliffs, surrounded by verdant flora.
  • The area is characterized by pristine natural beauty, with diverse wildlife, vibrant plant life, and clear flowing waters.

Recreational Activities:

  • Visitors can engage in a variety of activities such as hiking, trekking, zip-lining, and enjoying nature walks. The trails are well-marked, offering an immersive experience into the natural surroundings.
  • The park also provides opportunities for swimming in the natural pools formed by the waterfalls, a refreshing experience especially on warm days.

Amenities:

  • The park offers facilities to accommodate visitors, including resting areas and picnic spots where families can enjoy meals amidst nature.
  • Some areas may also host activities and programs designed to engage visitors of all ages.

Educational and Cultural Significance:

  • The park is not just a recreational spot; it also serves as a space for environmental education, highlighting the importance of nature conservation.
  • Local communities may engage in cultural programs, showcasing their heritage and promoting local crafts and products.
